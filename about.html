About

My name in Mandarin is “Huang Jia En,” which means "additional blessing," and my brother is nine years my senior. Go figure. I suppose “Whoopsie Huang” doesn’t quite have the same ring to it.

A more common term ascribed to me in this day and age is the term “extra,” partially in part to my boundless obsession with wordplay and narratives.

To explain, I am a byproduct of my Taiwanese heritage, Shanghainese upbringing, and American education.   Call it cultural homelessness or a "well rounded" global perspective, but regardless of which corner of the globe I inhabit, I have never been truly at home.  

This restlessness results in a genuine love of people and growing interest in storytelling, - whether it be the displaced refugee, rising YouTube star, trends in cosmetic surgery. I’ll throw my pen (or rather, keyboard, at it).

At the end of the day, I’m happy to see you here. Feel free to say hello at helloimjustina@gmail.com!
